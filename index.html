📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Navigate your character through the maze to collect cheese before time runs out! But beware - the cheese moves, just like in real life!

🕹️ Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Move your character
  • Space: Pause the game
  • Gamepad / USB Joystick: D-pad or stick to move, Start button to pause/resume — plug in before or during play
  • 2 Player: Player 1 uses Arrow Keys (or gamepad 1), Player 2 uses WASD (or gamepad 2) — race to grab each cheese first!

🧀 Cheese Mechanics

  • Cheese appears in random locations in the maze
  • The cheese will move periodically - watch for the warning!
  • Collect cheese before it moves to earn points
  • Different cheese types give different scores

⭐ Scoring

  • 🧀 Regular Cheese: 10 points
  • 🧀🧀 Double Cheese: 25 points
  • ✨ Golden Cheese: 50 points (rare!)
  • ⚡ Speed Bonus: Collect cheese quickly for extra points!
  • 🔥 Combo: Chain collections within 8 seconds for a growing bonus, up to +100%!

🐺 Enemy Mice

  • 🦇 Wanderer: Roams randomly - dangerous if you bump into it
  • 🦝 Thief: Chases you down and steals 15% of your score on contact, then flees
  • 🐺 Guardian: Patrols near the cheese to intercept you
  • Any enemy contact costs a life and knocks you back to your spawn corner

🛡️ Power-ups

  • 🛡️ Shield: Blocks the next enemy hit entirely
  • 🍎 Apple: Restores a life, or adds +15 seconds if already at full lives

💡 Tips

  • Watch the cheese status indicator - it warns when cheese is about to move
  • Plan your route ahead - don't just react, anticipate!
  • Each level gets harder - the maze changes, gains loops, and cheese moves faster
  • Losing all your lives ends the game immediately - watch for enemies!
  • "The only thing that is constant is change" - adapt or fall behind!

📚 The Lesson

Just like in Spencer Johnson's book, this game teaches us to:

  • Anticipate change
  • Adapt quickly
  • Enjoy the adventure
  • Be ready to change again and again
