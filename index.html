📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Navigate your character through the maze to collect cheese before time runs out! But beware - the cheese moves, just like in real life!

🕹️ Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Move your character
  • Space: Pause the game

🧀 Cheese Mechanics

  • Cheese appears in random locations in the maze
  • The cheese will move periodically - watch for the warning!
  • Collect cheese before it moves to earn points
  • Different cheese types give different scores

⭐ Scoring

  • 🧀 Regular Cheese: 10 points
  • 🧀🧀 Double Cheese: 25 points
  • ✨ Golden Cheese: 50 points (rare!)
  • ⚡ Speed Bonus: Collect cheese quickly for extra points!

💡 Tips

  • Watch the cheese status indicator - it warns when cheese is about to move
  • Plan your route ahead - don't just react, anticipate!
  • Each level gets harder - the maze changes and cheese moves faster
  • "The only thing that is constant is change" - adapt or fall behind!

📚 The Lesson

Just like in Spencer Johnson's book, this game teaches us to:

  • Anticipate change
  • Adapt quickly
  • Enjoy the adventure
  • Be ready to change again and again
